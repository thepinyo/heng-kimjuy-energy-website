# Product Overview

Heng Kimjuy Energy is a marketing website for a full-service auto repair garage ("อู่ เฮง กิมจุ้ย") located in Samut Prakan, Thailand (Theparak Rd. Km.7). The business has 2 branches.

## Purpose
Single-page brochure site showcasing services, photo gallery, customer reviews, branch locations with maps, and contact info (phone, LINE, Facebook).

## Target Audience
Local Thai customers seeking auto repair services. The site supports three languages: Thai (default), English, and Chinese.

## Key Sections
- Hero with live open/closed status based on working hours
- Services grid (oil change, engine repair, brakes, tires, A/C, general inspection)
- Photo gallery with lightbox viewer
- Customer reviews (horizontal scrolling carousel)
- Branch locations with embedded Google Maps
- Contact links (phone, LINE, Facebook)

## Business Context
- Working hours: Mon–Sat 08:30–18:30, closed Sunday
- Primary contact: phone and LINE messaging
- Social presence: Facebook page for reviews and photos
